Welcome to Mom’s Magic Threads, where creativity and craftsmanship come together.

TheThreadWizard

Donna Taylor, known as TheThreadWizard, is my amazing mom who has been passionate about sewing and embroidery for most of her life. With her state-of-the-art sewing machine, she can create intricate and beautiful designs, bringing your ideas to life with precision and artistry. It’s incredible to see her take a design and, after a few versions, bring it to fruition. While she can’t make a shirt or pants from scratch, she can take your existing garments and turn them into something personal and unique. From hats to shirts to pants, she can permanently etch an embroidered photo or design into your fabric, making it truly special.

Current Projects

One of our exciting projects is a prototype for a wallet pocket that attaches to a belt. This innovative design provides extra storage and convenience, especially for garments without pockets. It’s perfect for adding a functional and stylish touch to any outfit.

Services Offered

  • Custom Embroidery: Personalize your garments, accessories, or home decor with unique embroidery designs.
  • Bespoke Creations: From custom clothing to unique fabric accessories, if you can dream it, TheThreadWizard can make it.
  • Quilting: Donna also creates amazing quilts that combine beauty and functionality, perfect for gifts or adding a cozy touch to your home.
